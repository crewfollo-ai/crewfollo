Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

This page is a general template for an early-stage SaaS / digital product. CrewFollo AI should have this policy reviewed by a qualified lawyer before public launch or commercial use.

1. Overview

CrewFollo AI provides early-stage tools and workflows that help home service businesses respond to leads, collect job details, prepare replies, and organize follow-up. This Privacy Policy explains how we may collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website, contact us, apply for a pilot, or use our services.

2. Information we collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as your name, business name, trade, service area, email address, phone number, lead sources, and messages you submit through forms or email.

If you participate in a pilot or product workflow, we may process lead-related information you choose to provide, such as customer messages, job details, photos or photo requests, service location, urgency, timing, access notes, and follow-up status.

We may also collect basic website and device information, such as browser type, pages visited, approximate location derived from IP address, and usage activity, if analytics tools are added later.

3. How we use information

We may use information to respond to inquiries, evaluate pilot applications, provide and improve the product, prepare lead summaries and reply drafts, troubleshoot issues, communicate with users, and maintain the safety and reliability of the service.

We may use aggregated or de-identified information to understand product usage, improve onboarding, and plan future features.

4. How we share information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the website, email, forms, hosting, analytics, or product infrastructure. These providers are expected to use information only to provide services to us.

We may disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, to prevent abuse, or in connection with a business transfer such as a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

5. AI processing and customer data

CrewFollo AI may use AI systems to help draft replies, summarize leads, and organize customer information. Users are responsible for reviewing AI-generated content before sending it to customers, especially during the MVP stage.

6. Data retention and security

We keep information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer period is required by law or necessary for legitimate business purposes. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Your choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of information you provided, subject to legal and operational limitations. You may also choose not to provide certain information, though that may limit our ability to respond or provide pilot access.

8. Children's privacy

CrewFollo AI is intended for business users and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we may provide notice through the website or by other reasonable means.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to crewfolloai@gmail.com.
