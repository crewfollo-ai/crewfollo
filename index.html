The problem

Small contractors lose jobs when replies are slow.

When you're on a ladder, driving to a job, buying materials, or talking to another customer, new leads can sit unanswered. By the time you reply, the customer may have already booked someone else.

CrewFollo keeps the conversation moving by preparing fast replies, asking for the right job details, and organizing every lead into a clean summary.

How it works

How CrewFollo works

From first message to follow-up, CrewFollo helps keep every lead moving.

1

Capture

A new lead comes from Facebook, Google, Yelp, your website, SMS, or referral.

2

Qualify

CrewFollo asks the right questions: service type, photos, ZIP code, timing, urgency, and access details.

3

Summarize

The owner receives a clean lead summary with missing details and the recommended next step.

4

Follow up

CrewFollo prepares polite follow-up messages when customers go quiet.

5

Book estimate

The conversation moves toward a scheduled estimate or next action.
