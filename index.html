How CrewFollo AI works

A simple workflow for capturing leads, qualifying requests, and preparing the next step.

Capture

A customer calls or sends a message about a service job.

Qualify

The AI collects photos, address, timing, service type, and key job details.

Prepare

The contractor receives a clean summary and a professional suggested reply.

Follow Up

CrewFollo AI helps prevent leads from going cold with reminders and follow-up messages.

Why now

Local service businesses are moving online, but many still manage leads manually across phone calls, text messages, Google Business Profile, Yelp, Facebook, and other local platforms. AI can help small contractors respond faster without hiring a full-time receptionist.

Built from real field experience

CrewFollo AI is being built with direct experience from the home service industry. The product is designed around real problems contractors face every day: missed calls, unclear job details, slow replies, and lost follow-ups.

Early-stage product

CrewFollo AI is currently in early MVP development. We are testing workflows with local service businesses and building around real contractor needs, not generic chatbot use cases.

MVP roadmap
