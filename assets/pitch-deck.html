Problem

Small contractors lose jobs when they cannot reply fast enough.

Owners are busy

They are on ladders, driving, buying materials, or talking with another customer.

Leads need details

Customers often send incomplete requests with no photos, ZIP code, timing, or clear scope.

Follow-up slips

Good prospects go quiet and are easy to forget without a simple system.

Solution

CrewFollo keeps the lead conversation moving.

  • Prepare fast, professional replies for new leads.
  • Ask for photos, ZIP code, urgency, timing, and access details.
  • Summarize every lead into an owner-friendly next step.
  • Prepare polite follow-ups when customers stop responding.
