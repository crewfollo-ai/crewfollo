Overview

The product is organized around one job: turn new leads into clear next steps

Instead of acting like a generic chatbot, CrewFollo is designed around contractor intake. It asks for the job information that matters, helps the owner respond professionally, and keeps follow-up from falling through the cracks.

Key features

Built for speed-to-lead and cleaner intake

Fast first reply

Prepare a professional response as soon as a lead comes in, even if the owner is still working.

Photo request flow

Ask for photos or video before guessing at the scope of repair, cleaning, painting, or installation work.

Trade-specific questions

Collect service type, urgency, ZIP code, property details, timing, and access notes.

Lead summary

Turn scattered messages into a short summary the owner can review at a glance.

Suggested next step

Recommend whether to request details, schedule a call, book an estimate, or follow up later.

Owner approval

Keep the owner in control before replies are sent during the MVP stage.

Follow-up templates

Prepare polite reminders when customers stop responding after the first message.

Simple setup

Start with practical workflows before adding heavier CRM or calendar automation.
